Your Vehicle Management App can be installed on your iPhone, iPad, or Android device and will work like a native app!

## 📱 Installation on iPhone/iPad (iOS)

### Step 1: Open Safari Browser
⚠️ **Important:** You MUST use **Safari** browser on iOS. Chrome/Firefox won't work for installation.

1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone or iPad
2. Navigate to: `https://car-maintenance-78.preview.emergentagent.com`

### Step 2: Add to Home Screen

1. **Tap the Share button** at the bottom (iPhone) or top (iPad) of Safari
   - It looks like a square with an arrow pointing up: 📤

2. **Scroll down** and find "Add to Home Screen"
   - It has an icon that looks like: ➕□

3. **Tap "Add to Home Screen"**

### Step 3: Customize (Optional)

1. You'll see a preview of the app icon
2. The name "Gestión de Vehículo" will appear
3. You can edit the name if you want (e.g., "Mi Coche")
4. **Tap "Add"** in the top right corner

### Step 4: Launch the App

1. Go to your home screen
2. You'll see the app icon (🚗)
3. Tap it to launch - it opens fullscreen like a native app!
4. No browser bars, just your app ✨

## 🤖 Installation on Android (Chrome)

### Step 1: Open Chrome Browser

1. Open **Google Chrome** on your Android device
2. Navigate to: `https://car-maintenance-78.preview.emergentagent.com`

### Step 2: Install the App

**Option A: Using the Install Prompt (Easiest)**
1. A banner will appear at the bottom saying "Install app"
2. Tap **"Install"**
3. Confirm by tapping **"Install"** again
4. Done! The app icon appears on your home screen

**Option B: Using Chrome Menu (If no banner appears)**
1. Tap the **three dots menu** (⋮) in the top right corner
2. Tap **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**
3. In the popup, tap **"Install"**
4. The app will be added to your home screen

### Step 3: Launch the App

1. Go to your home screen or app drawer
2. Find the "Gestión de Vehículo" app icon
3. Tap it to launch - opens fullscreen!
4. Works just like a native Android app ✨

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "Add to Home Screen" option not showing (iOS)
- ✅ Make sure you're using **Safari** (not Chrome/Firefox)
- ✅ Check if you already installed it
- ✅ Refresh the page and try again
- ✅ Update iOS to latest version

### "Install app" option not showing (Android)
- ✅ Make sure you're using **Chrome** browser
- ✅ Try using the three dots menu → "Add to Home screen"
- ✅ Clear Chrome cache and try again
- ✅ Update Chrome to latest version

### App won't load after installation
- ✅ Check your internet connection
- ✅ Try force-closing and reopening
- ✅ On iOS: Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data
- ✅ On Android: Settings → Apps → Chrome → Clear Cache

### Login issues
- ✅ Make sure you have a Google account
- ✅ Allow popups in your browser
- ✅ Try logging out and back in
- ✅ Check internet connection

### Can't see my data
- ✅ Make sure you're logged in with the same Google account
- ✅ Pull down to refresh the screen
- ✅ Check internet connection
- ✅ Data syncs automatically when online
